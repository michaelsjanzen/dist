# Phase 2: ISOLATE (The Sort)

> "The next step is elemental separation: separating the silicon (mechanical) from the carbon (human)."

## The Concept
Once your role is dissolved into tasks, you must sort them based on the nature of the intelligence required. This creates two distinct lists: **AI-Ready Tasks** (Silicon) and **Human Responsibilities** (Carbon).

## The Categories

### Silicon (AI-Ready Tasks)
These are tasks that follow known patterns and use structured data. They are rigid and executable.
* **Characteristics:** Accuracy matters more than interpretation; rule-based; repetitive.
* **Examples:** Data entry, report generation, scheduling, basic code debugging.
* **Goal:** Automate or delegate to an AI agent.

### Carbon (Human Responsibilities)
These are organic tasks requiring judgment, ambiguity navigation, and relational trust.
* **Characteristics:** Reading between the lines; understanding unstated context; making calls with incomplete information.
* **Examples:** Persuading a skeptic, mentoring a team member, deciding *which* problem to solve.
* **Goal:** Expand and deepen these activities.
