# Phase 1: DISSOLVE (The Audit)

> "The first step is to apply that solvent consciously. You can stop treating your job as a solid block and start seeing it as a collection of elements."

## The Concept
Right now, your work likely presents itself as a solid block defined by a title (e.g., "Project Manager"). This block is actually a compressed bundle of hundreds of distinct tasks, activities, and micro-responsibilities.

AI acts as a solvent on this bundle. To manage this dissolution effectively, you must proactively break down the role yourself before the market does it for you.

## The Protocol
The goal of this phase is **granularity**. Memory is unreliable; it edits out the mechanical valleys of your day. To redesign your work, you need data.

### 1. The Activity Log
For one week (or at least 3 days), track every professional activity.
* **Avoid broad labels:** "Marketing" is useless.
* **Be specific:** "Resizing images for social media (45 mins)" vs. "Debating subject line (15 mins)" are distinct tasks requiring different skills.
* **Capture the invisible:** Note the time spent staring at a wall thinking (strategy) vs. time spent fighting formatting (drudgery).
